About TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.

About TEDxRMIT

Ranked in the Top 200 of the Best Global Universities by QS and a 5-star QS ranking for the quality of undergraduate education, RMIT University Melbourne is a globally recognized school for leadership skills and innovation. As the main campus of RMIT Melbourne University in Asia, after more than 20 years of establishment and development, RMIT University Vietnam constantly offers learning experiences in an international environment for more than 10,500 students.

TEDx RMIT Hanoi 2022 is an event within the framework of TEDx held at RMIT University in Hanoi, with the theme "TRANSITION: Waves of Breakthrough". In addition to the breakthrough in terms of the program in comparison to previous sessions and the original TED format, this theme also bears the interpretation that our event will prepare the audience for the transitions in life and motivate them to forge their own breakthroughs in the path of self-development and assertion.

With the sharing from the speakers who are highly influential individuals, the event promises to bring a new breeze to TEDx as well as spread positive changes in perception to the community.

Theme

In line with the motto "Ready for Life and Work" of RMIT University, TEDx RMIT 2022 is pleased to introduce the theme "TRANSITION: Waves of breakthrough" .

Waves represent transitions in life, whether big or small, optimistic or pessimistic, accidental or intentional, anticipated or unanticipated - the waves of possibilities. Waves are wild, untamed, free-moving forces with overwhelming power. Yet they are not entirely impossible to predict. If we can catch the waves, we can reach the ocean. But if we try to resist them, we will be pushed back to the shore.

Waves are also metaphors for internal emotions, thoughts, concerns, and aspirations that emerge when faced with transitions in one’s mind. Those waves become even stronger when the things we already know, the values ​​we have always held as the foundation, the plans we have set out for ourselves, are challenged by waves of change being formed and reinforced every day in society.

A person's life has meaning and structure because of the waves of change that occur in it and within themselves. The question therefore arises - How can we stay in tune with the inevitable currents of life while riding our own waves?
